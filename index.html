ABOUT ECLECTIC

Eclectic is a small, one-man operation; a publication of anything and everything. Eclectic yearns to be the home of the passionate, publishing material that is well-written, well-researched, and overall neglected or too archaic, labyrinthine, obsessive, and even grotesque or avant-garde. We find interest in the gritty, the unknown, the unknowable.

If you have a penchant to tell a story with obsessive and meticulous detail, Eclectic may just be the home for your work.
